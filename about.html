About School Jiffy

School Jiffy is a website made by three students from All-Star Codes intensive program. Our website allows parents to find the ideal private school for their child. We care that your child recevies the best education possible. Also we care that the schools are set at resonable prices so parents are able to afford for their child to attend. So at School Jiffy we provide a number of quality, well priced schools all around the Bronx.
